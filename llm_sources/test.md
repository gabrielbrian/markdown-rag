# Quick Cooking Guide

## Desserts

### Chocolate Chip Cookies

#### Ingredients

You'll need 2 1/4 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon baking soda, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 cup softened butter, 3/4 cup granulated sugar, 3/4 cup brown sugar, 2 eggs, 2 teaspoons vanilla extract, and 2 cups chocolate chips.

#### Instructions

Preheat oven to 375Â°F. Mix flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl. In another bowl, beat butter with both sugars until fluffy. Add eggs and vanilla. Gradually mix in flour mixture, then fold in chocolate chips. Drop spoonfuls onto baking sheets and bake for 9-11 minutes.

## Main Courses

### Chicken Tikka Masala

#### Overview

Chicken Tikka Masala is one of the most popular dishes in British and Indian cuisine. It features tender chunks of marinated chicken in a rich, creamy tomato sauce with aromatic spices.

#### Marinade

For 2 pounds of chicken, use 1 cup plain yogurt, 2 tablespoons lemon juice, 2 teaspoons garam masala, 1 teaspoon each of turmeric, cumin, and paprika.

#### Sauce

The sauce requires 2 tablespoons butter, 1 diced onion, 3 cloves minced garlic, 1 tablespoon grated ginger, one 28-ounce can crushed tomatoes, 1 cup heavy cream, and 2 teaspoons garam masala.

##### Cooking Method

Marinate chicken for at least 20 minutes. Grill or broil until slightly charred. Cook onions in butter until golden, add garlic and ginger, then tomatoes and spices. Simmer 10 minutes, add cream, then add chicken and simmer another 10 minutes. Serve with rice or naan.
